Meet the Team

Tom Fisher, DDS

Dr. Fisher is a graduate of both the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill and Ohio State University School of Dentistry. He is a qualified, trusted and informed dental practitioner. He is a member of the American Dental Association, Second District Dental Society and the Charlotte Dental Society.

Arnold Ma, DDS

Dr. Ma is a 1997 graduate of the Indiana University School of Dentistry and a member of both the American Dental Association and the Academy of General Dentistry. Dr. Ma has earned a certificate in hospital dentistry and holds a part-time faculty teaching position at Carolinas Medical Center.

Jenniffer - Hygienist

Jenniffer has been a dental hygienist with our practice for 1 year. Jenniffer grew up in Waxhaw and graduated from York Technical College in 2010. Jenniffer likes to spend time with her family and enjoys reading, taking trips, and lake activities

Beth - Hygienist

Beth has been a dental hygienist at our practice for 6 years. She graduated from York Technical College in South Carolina and has been a dental hygienist for 9 years. Beth was born and raised in Charlotte. Outside the office Beth enjoys spending time with her husband and children as well as her close friends. She also enjoys reading, cooking, and doing creative projects.

Caroline - Hygienist

Caroline has been a dental hygienist at our pracitce for 2 years. She graduated from Cape Fear Community College in 2009 and has been a dental hygienist for 3 years. Caroline was born and raised in Charlotte. Caroline has also received her degree in Spanish and Biology from The University of North Carolina at Wilmington. Caroline will be getting married in Sept 2012 and has a boxer named Harvey. She enjoys singing and outdoor activities.

Makiko - Hygienist

Amy - Dental Assistant

CEREC® One-Visit Crowns

CEREC® One-Visit Crowns


CEREC® is the world's only system for the fabrication of all ceramic dental restorations in one office visit. With CEREC®, we can complete your permanent, all ceramic crown in just one quick visit! This means fewer shots, less drilling, and less time out of your hectic schedule for dental care.

Implant Dentistry

Implant Dentistry
