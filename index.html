Meet the Team

Dr. Jim Coleman

Dr. Jim Coleman received his dental degree from the University of Missouri at Kansas City and then went on to complete a two-year Comprehensive Dental Residency at Bethesda Naval Hospital.

Dr. Coleman's accomplishments include the elite honor of becoming a Las Vegas Institute for Advanced Dental Studies specializes graduate. He also earned his fellowship and became a lifetime member in the World Congress of Minimally Invasive Dentistry. He is a long-standing member of the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry and gives back to the community through active participation in his church and the Rotary Club.

The Team

We enjoy working together and sharing our lives with our "family". Some of our team have been with us since day one and they are indeed like "family". We invite you to join us and experience excellent care from our "family" to yours.

Lynn Coleman - Office Manager

My name is Lynn Coleman and I am Dr. Coleman's wife of 45 years. We spent 21 years in the US Navy and during that time I worked as a clergy secretary. Since 1992 I have been Dr. Coleman's Office Manager. Some of our team have been with us since day one and they are like family.

Paula - Dental Hygienist

I received my BSDH from UW in 1977 and an MBA from Seattle U in 1989, and have been lucky to work with Dr. Coleman's team since 2007. I enjoy all aspects of building relationships in family dentistry, especially specializing in periodontal therapies and customizing the treatment options. I am continually challenged, encouraged and energized by Dr. Coleman to pursue excellence on our patient's behalf. Personally, I am blessed to be the mom of three kids with two currently in college. I enjoy gardening, chasing the sun, reading and wine tasting. I am very thankful to all of you for choosing us for your dental care!

Tina - Scheduling Coordinator

Working with Dr. Coleman is constant education, and I wouldn't want it any other way. I am thrilled to participate in beyond "just teeth" dentistry, wellness and working with my sister Venesa. I especially enjoy talking with our patients, sharing life's experiences and building trust. Away from the office, I love spending time with my family, paper-crafting, container gardening and the beach rain or shine.

CEREC® One-Visit Crowns

CEREC® One-Visit Crowns


CEREC® is the world's only system for the fabrication of all ceramic dental restorations in one office visit. With CEREC®, we can complete your permanent, all ceramic crown in just one quick visit! This means fewer shots, less drilling, and less time out of your hectic schedule for dental care.

Implant Dentistry

Implant Dentistry

Digital Radiography

Family Care
