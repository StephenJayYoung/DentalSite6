Meet the Team

Dr. Jim Coleman

Dr. Jim Coleman received his dental degree from the University of Missouri at Kansas City and then went on to complete a two-year Comprehensive Dental Residency at Bethesda Naval Hospital.

Dr. Coleman's accomplishments include the elite honor of becoming a Las Vegas Institute for Advanced Dental Studies specializes graduate. He also earned his fellowship and became a lifetime member in the World Congress of Minimally Invasive Dentistry. He is a long-standing member of the American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry and gives back to the community through active participation in his church and the Rotary Club.

Services We Offer

Click on any service below to learn more about our offerings.

Family Care

Family Care


From toddlers to the elderly, our skilled dental team is familiar with the unique needs of every patient, no matter their age. Our goal is to help them develop great oral habits that will last them a lifetime.

Digital X-rays

Digital X-rays


Our use of digital x-ray technology reduces your radiation exposure and eliminates the use of chemicals. It’s better for you and the environment.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic Dentistry


Cosmetic dentistry is one of the most cost-effective ways to improve your appearance. A beautiful, bright, healthy smile can increase self confidence and overall life satisfaction.

Sedation Dentistry

Sedation Dentistry


Sedation dentistry is an excellent treatment for patients with extensive dental needs, or who are concerned about anxiety, difficulty getting numb, or sensitive teeth.

One-Visit Crowns

One-Visit Crowns


CEREC® is the world's only system for the fabrication of all ceramic dental restorations in one office visit. With CEREC®, we can complete your permanent, all ceramic crown in just one quick visit! This means fewer shots, less drilling, and less time out of your hectic schedule for dental care.

Cosmetic Bonding

Cosmetic Bonding


Composite resin is a white, tooth colored material that can be shaded to match your teeth. This material can give you a low-cost improvement in your smile.

Veneers

Veneers


Veneers are very thin porcelain pieces that are custom made to adhere directly to the front of teeth without grinding or shaving. Transform your smile without drilling on your natural teeth!

Composite Fillings

Composite Fillings


The common silver filling contains mercury. Dr. Jim Coleman uses the highest-quality biocompatible, tooth-colored restorations to create healthy, beautiful smiles.

Prevention and Gum Care

Prevention and Gum Care


By actively preventing disease and decay through regular home care, professional dental cleanings and regular exams, you will maintain a healthy, beautiful smile for life.

Ozone Therapy

Ozone Therapy


Medical ozone is a mixture of oxygen and ozone and has been shown to be an effective anti-bacterial, anti-fungal and anti-viral treatment agent. The treatment also stimulates healing, reduces inflammation and helps fight infections.

The WAND

The WAND


Patient comfort is important to us, so we are proud to offer the latest technology in providing anesthetic. The WAND makes the injection more comfortable for the patient as it emits a balanced flow of anesthetic through the tissue.

Laser Technology

Laser Technology


This technology allows us to use laser-energized water to cut the teeth instead of other drilling methods. The lasers can aid in soft and hard-tissue procedures by enhancing comfort and improve precision.
